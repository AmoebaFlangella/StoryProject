Prisoner Introduction

You awaken to find yourself in a jail cell. The cell is made of stone brick, with your only source of light coming from beyond the bars of the cell
You've been here so long that you don't even remember what crime you committed to get locked up in here.
Though, you do know that you've been here long enough to come up with an escape plan

During last weeks dinner, you managed to get a makeshift lockpick from one of the prisoners by using your hard-earned favors.
You also managed to get a metal bar that you hid underneath your bed.

How do you want to escape?
