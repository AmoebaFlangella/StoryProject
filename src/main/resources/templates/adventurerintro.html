Adventurer Introduction

You've always loved being an adventurer, a free spirit. You've gone to lands close and far, though, as of recent, you found a new calling - the sea.
For one reason or another, the crashing of waves, the promise of distant lands it calls to you like a sirens song.


You decided to quench this thirst and traveled to Arovorth - a coastal city in the Dyrsthrom Empire.
You've been here before, once or twice, though, it was mostly just as a stop on a bigger journey.
People here tend to be a bit suspicious of outsider, especially towards Orcs, so you never had the need to stay longer than required, today being no exception.


Upon coming to the harbor, you notice two available ships.
One - a small fishing boat and the other a trading ship.
While the fishing boat would be a lot cheaper, it'd also be less safe.
The trading ship, however, would be more expensive, but you'd have the protection of the merchants and their guards on board.

Which do you choose?
