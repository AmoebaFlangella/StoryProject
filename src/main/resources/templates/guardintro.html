Guard Introduction

You guard the village of Orswen from the outer wilds and various bandits. Being on the edge of the Kingdom, it's very vulnerable to various bandits. You're stationed at the front gate, a long with a few other guards, all of you doing your best to protect your own.


Most days at Orswen were incredibly calm, though, today was not one of those days. In the distance, you see a rising pillar of black smoke. Black smoke, in these parts, usually indicates an incoming bandit warband.
