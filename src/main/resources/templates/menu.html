Welcome to the Land of Dead Gods

Welcome to the main menu for the text-based browser game- Land of Dead Gods.

Land of Dead Gods is a text-based, choose-your-own-adventure style web-browser based game.
While the story takes some inspirations from various media, the core of it is a completely original story.
This game is mainly a school project, however, I hope to work on it on-and-off and, hopefully, turn it into a full game at some point.
