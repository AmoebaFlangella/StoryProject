The Fishing Boat

Upon considering your options, you decide to go for the cheapest one - the fishing boat
Your "ship" is roughly large enough to fit two people and, while not as big as a ship,
it's definitely still bigger than just a normal boat. You walk up to it and see an elderly man
bringing in some nets. He looks over at you and shouts "!"

You get somewhat surprised, though, as you look at him more, you can see that it's an old acquaintance
you met on one of your trips to the Empire. If you recall correctly, his name was Darius.
