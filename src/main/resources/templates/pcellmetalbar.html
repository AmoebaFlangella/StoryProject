Using the Metal Bar

You get out of your bed and flip the mattress up. Reaching underneath, you pull out the metal bar you'd hidden there prior.
Luckily for you, the guards in this prison are exceptionally bad and fail to notice prisoners smuggling things.
You walk up to the metal-bar-door and wedge the pipe in it, pulling as hard as you can. After a second or two, with a loud "CLUNK", the rusty door's lock breaks and the door swings open.
The noise of the door hitting the wall lets out a loud noise, alerting the guards as you can hear sounds of confusion coming from the distance.
